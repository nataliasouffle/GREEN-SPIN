Product's function

Our product is a protein powder made of organic spinach and eggs only. This two ingredients went through a prosses of dehydration to achived the green powder we present you. This green podwer will give you the protein necessary to achive the goals you desire.

Procedure

To create our protein powder we begin with seleccion of good quality egg whites and spinach.

The egg whites undergo a strict process involving separation, and dehydration, resulting in a fine powder that still keeps the full value of the protein.S

After that, the fresh spinach is carefully washed. dried. and transformed into a powder form, making sure its nutritional value remains intact.

The powder egg and spinach are mixed together and we have our final product
